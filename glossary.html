Glossary

Stocks

Ask Price

The minimum price a seller is willing to receieve.

Bearish

Expecting the price to fall.

Beta

The measure of an asset's risk in relation to the market (example S&P500) or to an alternative benchmark or factors.

Bid Price

The maximum price a buyer is willing to pay.

Bullish

Expecting the price to rise.

Earnings per share (EPS)

A company's profit divided by its number of common outstanding shares.

Market Capitalization

The total dollar value of all outstanding shares. It is computed as shares times current market price.

Market Prices

The Amount of Money that a willing buyer pays to accurite something from a willing seller.

For more Stock related terms please visit the NASDAQ Glossary.

Crypto

Address

A crypto address is a string of characters that represents a waller that can send and recieve cryptocurrency.

Block

A file containing information on transactions completed during a given time period. Blocks are the constituent parts of a blockchain.

Memecoin

A crypto token created as a joke or meme and claim to offer huge gains to holders.

Scamcoin

Coins that are created as a get rich quick schemes.
