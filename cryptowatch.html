Dogecoin (DOGE)

Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency created by Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer, who decided to create Dogecoin as a joke. It soon became known as the 1st meme coin and the 1st dog coin. Dogecoin is based on Scrypt algorithm, and the transaction process is more convenient than Bitcoin. For example, Dogecoin takes 1 minute to confirm, when Bitcoin takes about 10 minutes. Dogecoin is very popular coin due to the fact that it speread around the world so fast.
