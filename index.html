On our website you will be given the tools you need to make informed decisions when looking to invest. The liveticker has the ability to give you a live view of how a stock or cryptocurrency is doing currently.

As a side note, this is not financial advice we are computer science majors not finance majors. The information you can find on this website is based purely on our speculation as amateur (very amateur) investors. Here you will read about the various pages on this website and what they're for:

Liveticker

Crypto
